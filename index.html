The Mirror Pairs

Every piece on one side has a counterpart on the other.
Each pair asks the same question differently.

Reason's pieces are things — architecture, machines, patterns, texts.
Mystery's pieces are acts — suffering, bearing, sheltering, calling, surrendering, offering.
One side you could photograph. The other you have to experience.
